Between 6 and 14 percent of all displaced women of reproductive age (15-49 years) could be pregnant at any given time. Fifteen percent of women who are pregnant while fleeing from conflict may experience a life-threatening obstetric complication like the postpartum hemorrhage.

Women and girls make up around 50 per cent of any refugee, internally displaced or stateless population

A baby is being born to a refugee fleeing the civil war in Syria every hour.
