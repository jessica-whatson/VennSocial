Code Red


A charity for women by women.

How is our Charity different to others?

We have created this charity because we believe that one of the most significant barriers to donation is too much choice. Common questions when donating such as 'How much should I give?' or 'Should I support a specific program?' means that the donation process is fraught with choice and options. Code Red eliminates this paradox of choice by linking users straight to an exisiting wishlist on Amazon made by us, as women, to the products we believe are basic products that any woman needs.

Why donate?

Every woman should have the ability to maintain her dignity! This is our mission - to restore dignity and self-respect to thousands of women.

For the price of a coffee a day, you could make a huge difference to someones life.

Who are we?

Rosie, Jess and Olga are students on the Code First Girls course. We care about other women and believe that women everywhere should have access to feminine products.

We are using our platform to raise awareness of the importance of feminine products, and to provide women with the basics product that they need.
